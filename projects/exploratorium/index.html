Exploratorium

The page offers an exploratory insight into groups of individuals from the “History of Islām” (Taʾrīḫ al-islām) of al-Ḏahabī who share common ‘descriptors’ (sing. nisbaŧ). By selecting a ‘descriptor’ (), you can get preliminary analyses of geographical, chronological, and network patterns.


Space

The spatial pattern: the larger the dot, the more scholars who are qualified by the selected ‘descriptor’ are associated with the location.

Time




Chronological pattern: absolute and relative frequencies over time.
